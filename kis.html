KIS COURSE GUIDE

In 2018, the administrative body of Korea International School (KIS) commissioned our Design & Innovation Department to completely revamp the course selection guide. The head of the department, Mr. Jacob McCullough, selected me to work with administrators for three months to deliver the final result. My new course guide can be seen on the KIS website.
