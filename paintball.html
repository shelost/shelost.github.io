ITHACA PAINTBALL

Ithaca Paintball is a locally owned business, the go-to destinaton for everything from parties to casual hangouts. I created both a logo and business cards for IP.

THE LOGO

The logo is modeled after graffiti font, one of the most ubiquitous icons of paintball. When coupled with the paint splatters in the background, the text resembles what one might find on the walls of a New York subway.
