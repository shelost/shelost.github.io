NYBC MARKETING

The New York Bible Conference (NYBC) is held every year in January, bringing four churches from upstate NY together for three days of seminars, wo rship, and more. I designed marketing materials for the 2020 conference.

CONCERT POSTER

Students from the Eastman School of Music host a fundraising concert for NYBC every year.

CONCERT TICKETS

The tickets were designed to be bought in pairs, so that people would bring a friend along.

MAIN POSTER

Each NYBC conference has a distinct biblical theme, and the 2020 theme was "Heaven and the Fisher's Net." I created the poster so that the fish would be drawn to the light, which was one of the core principles of the conferences' founding.
