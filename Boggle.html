Boggle

My First JS / HTML Project

Boggle is one of my favorite games, and its simple mechanics made it an
ideal choice for my first Javascript game. The rules are simple:
Find as many words as you can within the time limit (usually 2-3 minutes)!
Words can connect through any adjacent boxes.
